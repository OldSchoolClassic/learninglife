Scheduling Details

Scheduling opens mid-April each year. I send a Google Booking link in late March/early April to families I've connected with previously. If you don't see the email, feel free to reach out or use the link below.
  • All evaluations conducted virtually via Google Meet — no travel needed
  • Statewide — all Pennsylvania counties welcome
  • Evaluations typically take place May through June
  • You do not need to be completely finished with instruction to schedule
  • Most evaluations last 30–60 minutes per child
  • If you have more than two children, please schedule additional appointments

Evaluation Prep Checklist

Bring these to your evaluation (or have them ready to share your screen):

  • Child present (brief student interview required by PA law)
  • Samples from each required subject showing progress over the year
  • Reading log (titles read or listened to)
  • Standardized assessment results (3rd, 5th, and 8th grade only)
  • Evaluation fee: $40 first child · $25 each sibling
  • Optional: any questions you have for me
12th Grade Evaluations
If you are seeking a 12th grade evaluation and diploma signature, please also bring transcripts and course descriptions (or portfolio samples) for all required secondary subjects. Credits begin to count towards graduation in 7th grade.
