Schedule an Evaluation

Choose a convenient time for your homeschool evaluation through an easy, flexible booking system.

Book Now — Google Calendar
Important: Pennsylvania requires the child to be present during the evaluation. However, they do not need to stay for the entire discussion.

Scheduling Information

When: I begin scheduling evaluations in mid-April. I conduct evaluations throughout May and June. I will send an email with a Google Booking link in late March/early April to families I've connected with before. If you don't see the email, feel free to reach out or use the booking link directly.

Where: All evaluations are conducted virtually via Google Meet. You'll receive a meeting link in your confirmation email. You do not need a Google account to join — just click the link at your appointment time.

How long: Most evaluations last 30–60 minutes per child. If you have more than two children, please schedule additional appointments.

Note: You do not need to be completely finished with instruction for the year to complete your evaluation. The evaluation should happen near the end of your school year — usually mid-April through June.

Book Your Evaluation

Key Details

Deadline

Submit your evaluation form to your superintendent or umbrella school by June 30. If you're late, they will contact you before taking any further action — don't panic.

Fee

$40 for the first child · $25 for each sibling. Payment is due after I send you the evaluation form. Options: Zelle, Venmo (@Alona-Cortes), PayPal (@alonajeansnyder), Apple Cash.

All PA Counties

All evaluations are virtual, so I can work with families statewide — every Pennsylvania county.

After the Evaluation

I'll email you the evaluation form with instructions. Submit it to your superintendent. Keep a copy for your records — many families add it to their child's portfolio.

Evaluation Prep Checklist

  • Child present for a brief student interview (does not need to stay the whole time)
  • Portfolio samples for each required subject showing progress over the year
  • Reading log (list of books/texts read or listened to)
  • Standardized assessment results — 3rd, 5th, and 8th grade only
  • $40 fee for first child · $25 per sibling (paid after you receive the form)
  • Optional: A list of any questions you have for me

Portfolio samples can include worksheets, workbook pages, projects, project photos, ticket stubs for events, writing samples, video clips — anything demonstrating progress. Digital portfolios (Google Drive, slideshows) are welcome.
